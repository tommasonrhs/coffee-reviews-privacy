Privacy Policy for Coffee Reviews

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Introduction

Coffee Reviews ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (for account creation and login)
  • Username (displayed on your reviews)
  • Password (encrypted and stored securely)

Location Data

  • We access your location only when you use the "Near Me" map feature
  • Location data is used only to show nearby restaurants
  • We do not store or track your location history

User-Generated Content

  • Restaurant reviews (ratings, comments)
  • Restaurants you add to the app

How We Use Your Information

  • Account Management: To create and maintain your account
  • App Functionality: To display your reviews and username
  • Location Services: To show restaurants near you when requested
  • Service Improvement: To fix bugs and improve the app experience

Data Storage

  • All data is stored securely using Supabase (a trusted database service)
  • Passwords are encrypted
  • We follow industry-standard security practices

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Your reviews are publicly visible within the app

Your Rights

  • Access: You can view your data in the app
  • Deletion: Contact us to delete your account and data
  • Username Changes: You can change your username once every 30 days

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication hosting
  • Google Places API: Restaurant search and location data
  • Sentry: Crash reporting (no personal data is sent)

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date.
